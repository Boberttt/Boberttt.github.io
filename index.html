What is a Beamer?

Beamers are unskilled individuals who usually use pre-made malware in order to infect victims. Since these are not remotely skilled people, they usually use stealers that only run, get data, send data to c2, and exit.

The Story (November 2022)

I was on Discord looking at some servers, one message caught my eye, "if anyone wants to learn how to hack, DM me". I thought that message was suspicious and decided to send a message to them to see what they were up to. They asked if I wanted a stealer builder, when they sent the download link, I didn't waste my time doing static anylasis, I uploaded the "builder" to bazaar.abuse.ch (a mass sandboxing website) and waited. After waiting a few hours I looked at some of the sandboxes, and the network traffic was very revealing. I was able to see the discord webhook used by the malware to deliver stolen data. I started spamming the webhook with messages such as "unpacked by coffee&shells".

After the beamer realized that I got access to his webhook, he invited me to join his server (I don't know why). The server was about beaming other people using the owners payloads. The way it worked was when someone ran the payload, the whole server was able to see the dumped credentials via discord webhook. The server had about 20 members consisting mostly of people who he beamed (again, I don't know why). I used the webhook to delete legiment logs (just two). I've also constantly reported the download links, server(s), and users.

How do I not get hacked?

Don't be dumb, don't click random links that strangers or friends send you. And have a decent anti-virus, like bitdefender.
